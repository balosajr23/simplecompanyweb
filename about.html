ABOUT OUR COMPANY


Kalakosyana Ramen House was founded by John Ray Balosa in February 13, 2023. It currently resides in ApolSam, Quezon City with only one branch up-to-date. Kalakosyana Ramen House is striving to be the best ramen serving restaurant in Quezon City, and hopeful to further its branches in every part of the Philippines.
